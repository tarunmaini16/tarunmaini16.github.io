Upalabdh

उपलब्ध - As it means "availability" in Hindi Language.
This extension is to make user aware about the "out of stock / unavailable / undeliverable" products as soon as they are available for purchase.
This was created while experiencing the need to solve problem of placing orders as soon as they arrive in store at this PEAK time
As of now it supports Amazon, Flipkart & BigBasket. You will not miss to purchase products saved in your cart anymore.

Download from Chrome Webstore           Contribute

Features

  1. Allows to save list of products.
  2. Receive notifications when the product status changes.
  3. Coloured indicators to show status of product.
  4. Ability to add/delete products.
  5. Supports Amazon, Flipkart and BigBasket.

Add New Product

User can add new product in new list by just clicking "ADD" button at top center.
Added product will be displayed in list with status as "New".

Delete Product

User can delete saved product by just clicking on red bin icon at right corresponding to product and item will vanish.

Push Notifications

If the status of any product changes, user will receive notifications corresponding to that product and notification indicator will change status accordingly.

How to USE

  • Click on ADD button at center to add any product after navigating to required product page.
  • The product on page will be added under list with Blue color status, indicating as newly added product.
  • WithIn 5-7 seconds, the color status of that product will change to actual status.
  • You can add as many products as you want.
  • To remove any product from list, click on red BIN icon present at right corresponding to product, it will be removed after confirmation.
  • That's all enjoy receiving notifications corresponding to status change in any product.
  • Supports Amazon, Flipkart and BigBasket as of now

FAQ

  • Does this extension stores any user data and send it across network? NO
  • Can we increase the status check polling frequency? YES, as of now Upalabdh checks each product status after every 30 minutes, to change it follow instructions below:
    • Right Click on Extension Icon > Choose Manage Extensions
    • Toggle the "Developer Mode" to "ON"
    • Click on background page
    • Navigate to Console tab
    • Set variable `set_interval` = toNumberOfMinutesYouWantToCheck x 60 , as it takes seconds e.g for 60 minutes, set it as 3600 (in seconds)
  • Newly added product is not displaying correct name. It may occur due to the page url you are on, please copy the product name, paste it in search bar of E-Store, Open the product page and then ADD.
  • If you want to contribute, feel free to connect.
  • Supports Amazon, Flipkart and BigBasket as of now.
